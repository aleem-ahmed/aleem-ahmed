## Aleem's Chicken Penne

---

### Ingredients
- 2 onions, chopped
- 1 lb chicken breast
- Olive oil
- Goya Adobo seasoning (to taste)
- Black pepper (to taste)
- Paprika (to taste)
- Bertolli marinara sauce
- Cream of chicken
- 1 (6 oz) can Tuttorosso tomato paste
- Penne pasta
- Mozzarella cheese (generous amount)
- Ricotta cheese (generous amount)
- Water (about 1.5 cups)
- Fresh parsley, for garnish

### Instructions
1. Heat a cast iron pan with olive oil.
2. Add the chopped onions.
3. Fry until they are slightly blackened, then remove from pan and set aside.
4. Season the chicken breast with Goya Adobo and black pepper.
5. Cook the chicken breast in the cast iron pan until fully cooked and well-seared.
6. Chop the cooked chicken into bite-sized cubes.
7. In a separate pot, add Bertolli marinara sauce.
8. Add the cooked onions and cubed chicken to the sauce.
9. Stir in cream of chicken and one 6 oz can of Tuttorosso tomato paste.
10. Mix thoroughly and cook slowly over low heat until well combined and heated through.
11. Cook penne pasta according to package instructions.
12. Drain the penne pasta and set aside.
13. Add the sauce (with chicken and onions) to the drained pasta and mix until evenly coated.
14. Add a generous amount of mozzarella cheese and ricotta cheese.
15. Pour in about 1.5 cups of water.
16. Stir well and continue cooking gently until the cheese is melted and everything is fully combined.
17. Finish by adding fresh parsley on top as a garnish.

---

### Notes
- Onions were intentionally cooked until lightly charred for deeper flavor.
- The dish is creamy, cheesy, and slightly spicy from paprika.
- Adjust seasoning or add additional spices to taste.
